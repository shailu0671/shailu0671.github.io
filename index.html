WELCOME TO OUR SCHOOL

Established in 1928, St. Raphael's Higher Secondary School is one of the oldest, private unaided minority educational institution in Indore. It is dedicated to the service of fostering intellectual and personal development among the young learners and grooming them to be committed and competent individuals. This catholic Institution is administered by the Sisters of the Holy spirit (SSpS). This catholic Institution is administered by the Sisters of the Holy spirit (SSpS).
